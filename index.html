Gregorio, Keith Elly V.

I face many problems with understanding myself, so in order to introduce myself, I've asked the people that I've been friends with over the years of my life, and I am forever thankful of them.
Especially Anki who's been with me since I was 4.

Creator has always been someone reliable. They're intellectual, creative, and has a somewhat complex personality. Keith is the friend who listens to everyone else's problems, but always keeps their own tightly locked away. Even when they finally manage to open up, it would never be their full story. They've been through alot and we are all thankful that they're still with us as of today. Keith absolutely loves complex narratives and/or storylines, have the darkest sense of humor, and has always been an empathic person. Even if they hide it behind sarcasm, detachment, or even the uncontrolled chaos they bring everywhere. - Anon

She's always been an open book when it comes to the most contraversial topics. reading explicit stories out in the open, or even go as far as listening to something suggestive. Keith has been a big part of our lives, she's both the light that drags us out of misery but also the darkness that consumes our bright joy. She's a sharp person and observes everything, even if she's not herself. She overanalyzes everything and can read people like an open book. She's the type of person to avoid people when she's spiraling. She knows how it feels like to carry a heavy weight. She's been pushed away by people she trusted but she's still here, alive and well. She uses humor and sarcasm to hide from people, she tends to be blunt but we can tell that she cares alot.
She has a strong side to herself but that also makes her incredibly hard on herself. But we're happy to have been friends with her over the coarse of half of our lives. She's a loyal person, regardless, of how hurt she is. Exactly the type to love a person so deeply but also moves on as easily as a switch. - Anki
